Jessica Leach

Jessica studied Biomedical Science before teaching and working in elearning and edtech. She has made illustrations for The Guardian and a comic book about Schizophrenia, with funding from the UK Arts Council. This summer she is a participant in the Rails Girls Summer of Code Open Source programme.

Denise Kehoe

Denise Kehoe completed her Art and Design degree with 1st Class honours. In 2011, she was awarded the Art Trail Graduate award, the CIT purchase prize and the CIT President's award and was nominated to represent Ireland at the Neu now festival in Estonia. Her influences range from puns, pizza, picasso and pictionary.
