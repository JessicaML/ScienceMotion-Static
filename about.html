About us

Jessica Leach and Denise Kehoe met while teaching English as a foreign language to uncooperative teenagers, trying to distract them from their phones by making bad jokes.

Frustrated at the lack of teaching resources that really engaged students, and sharing an interest in animation and irreverent humour, they decided to build ScienceMotion after learning to code.

Science is about how shapes move in space, so we think animation is the best way to explain it... and we want to explain it in a way that's as fun to look at as MTV.

Jessica Leach studied Biomedical Science before teaching and working in elearning and edtech. She has made illustrations for The Guardian and a comic book about Schizophrenia, with funding from the UK Arts Council. This summer she is a participant in the Rails Girls Summer of Code Open Source programme.
